How to Use This Tool

Fill in the details about your car in the form above. Make sure to provide accurate information to get the best estimate of your car's value.

For example:

  • Car Company: Choose the brand of your car.
  • Manufacturing Year: Select the year your car was manufactured.
  • Kilometers Driven: Enter the total kilometers your car has been driven.
  • Fuel Type: Select the type of fuel your car uses.
  • Seller Type: Indicate whether you are selling as an individual or a dealer.
  • Transmission: Choose between manual or automatic transmission.
  • Owner: Select how many previous owners the car has had.
